Information

Bagel Tuesdays are our bread-and-butter event. Held bi-weekly or as frequently as possible, in various rooms. Write to us to get on our mailing list, or check our event schedule.

Bagels, spreads, and coffee will be provided at the venue.

The lecture usually runs about 20-40 minutes starting at 11:30, followed by a question or discussion period. Previous lecturers have included professors from our departments at Dalhousie and Kings, a visiting professor from Leeds University, and graduate students. Write to us if you would like to present at a bagel tuesday.
